About Me

"If I could say it with words, there would be no reason to paint." Edward Hopper

I'm a 26-year-old Portuguese, visual arts undergraduate from Torres Vedras, a historic town in the western region of Portugal, known for its arts scene, natural beauty, and lively festivals, including the famous Carnival of Torres Vedras. My hometown has played a significant role in shaping my passion for creativity.
Throughout my career, I've gained extensive experience in the hospitality industry. Over the past eight years, I've worked in a variety of roles that have helped me develop great customer service skills, strong management abilities, and the ability to work effectively as part of a team.

Recently, I took on a new challenge as an executive assistant for a medicinal cannabis start-up. In this role, I'm responsible for coordinating the company's day-to-day operations, managing schedules and providing support to senior leadership.
I'm proud to say that my positive attitude, organisational skills, and ability to work collaboratively have all helped me thrive in this dynamic and fast-paced environment.
